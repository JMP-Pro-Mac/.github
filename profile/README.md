## What is JMP Pro for Mac?  

**JMP Pro for Mac** is the advanced edition of JMP, the statistical discovery software developed by SAS. It is designed for researchers, scientists, engineers, and business analysts who need powerful yet intuitive tools to handle complex data.  

With JMP Pro, you can:  
- Explore data with interactive visualizations  
- Build predictive models using regression, decision trees, and neural networks  
- Conduct advanced statistical analysis including ANOVA, multivariate analysis, and clustering  
- Design experiments (DOE) for scientific and industrial applications  
- Perform quality control and reliability analysis  
- Use simulation and risk modeling to support decision-making  

Unlike traditional programming-heavy environments, JMP Pro emphasizes a **point-and-click interface**, making it accessible to both beginners and advanced statisticians. For Mac users, it is fully optimized for Intel and Apple Silicon processors, ensuring smooth performance and integration with macOS.  

Whether you are conducting academic research, developing pharmaceutical products, or analyzing business performance, **JMP Pro for Mac** provides the advanced analytics environment you need.
